GFC Therapy (Growth Factor Concentrate)

An advanced regenerative treatment that uses purified growth factors extracted from your own blood to stimulate cell repair and natural regeneration.

GFC for Hair

  • Reduces hair fall
  • Stimulates new hair growth
  • Improves hair density & thickness
  • Strengthens hair follicles

GFC for Skin

  • Improves skin texture and glow
  • Reduces fine lines and wrinkles
  • Helps in skin rejuvenation and repair

Benefits

  • Safe & natural
  • Minimal downtime
  • Painless procedure
  • Long-lasting results

PRP Therapy (Platelet-Rich Plasma)

Uses your own blood platelets to stimulate healing and regeneration, widely used for hair loss and skin vitality.

PRP for Hair

  • Reduces hair fall
  • Stimulates dormant hair follicles
  • Improves hair volume

PRP for Skin

  • Improves skin quality
  • Reduces fine lines
  • Helps with acne scars

Benefits

  • Natural & safe
  • No chemicals
  • Minimal recovery time
