Clinical Solutions

GFC Therapy

Advanced regenerative treatment for hair & skin

Click for details

GFC Therapy (Growth Factor Concentrate)

An advanced regenerative treatment that uses purified growth factors extracted from your own blood to stimulate cell repair and natural regeneration.

GFC for Hair

  • Reduces hair fall
  • Stimulates new hair growth
  • Improves hair density & thickness
  • Strengthens hair follicles

GFC for Skin

  • Improves skin texture and glow
  • Reduces fine lines and wrinkles
  • Helps in skin rejuvenation and repair

Benefits

  • Safe & natural
  • Minimal downtime
  • Painless procedure
  • Long-lasting results

Chemical Peel

Dermatological treatments that exfoliate using medical-grade solutions to improve skin tone and clarity. Customized based on your specific concern.

Treats

  • Acne & acne scars
  • Pigmentation & melasma
  • Uneven skin tone
  • Dull & aging skin

Benefits

  • Brighter, smoother skin
  • Reduced pigmentation
  • Controlled acne
  • Minimal downtime (depending on type)

PRP Therapy

Natural treatment for hair regrowth & rejuvenation

Click for details

PRP Therapy (Platelet-Rich Plasma)

Uses your own blood platelets to stimulate healing and regeneration, widely used for hair loss and skin vitality.

PRP for Hair

  • Reduces hair fall
  • Stimulates dormant hair follicles
  • Improves hair volume

PRP for Skin

  • Improves skin quality
  • Reduces fine lines
  • Helps with acne scars

Benefits

  • Natural & safe
  • No chemicals
  • Minimal recovery time

About Us

At Dermafix24, we believe aesthetic care is deeply personal. Our clinic is built on the philosophy of enhancing natural beauty through safe, precise, and medically guided treatments.

We combine advanced technology with a thoughtful, patient-centric approach to deliver results that look refined, balanced, and effortless.Every face tells a story — our role is to help you feel confident in yours.

Our Mission

To redefine aesthetic care by offering globally inspired, evidence-based treatments that prioritise safety, subtlety, and long-term skin health. We aim to create an environment where patients feel informed, comfortable, and truly cared for — at every step of their journey.

Our Approach

Aesthetic treatments at Dermafix24 are never one-size-fits-all. Each consultation is carefully planned, each treatment thoughtfully customised, and each result designed to complement your natural features.

Our Commitment

Common Questions

Why Choose Dermafix 24? ▼

• Dermatologist-supervised treatments
• Advanced USFDA-approved technology
• Customized skin & hair solutions
• Safe, hygienic & ethical practice
• Visible, long-lasting results

Are these treatments safe? ▼

Yes. All treatments at Dermafix24 are performed under dermatological supervision using clinically proven and safe techniques.

Is there any downtime after treatments? ▼

Most treatments have minimal to no downtime. Some procedures like chemical peels or lasers may require short recovery depending on intensity.

How many sessions are required? ▼

The number of sessions varies depending on your skin or hair concern. A personalized treatment plan is created after consultation.

Is consultation mandatory? ▼

Yes. Consultation helps us understand your concern and recommend the most suitable treatment for you.
